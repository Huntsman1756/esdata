# ESData Contract Reference

ESData MCP answers are retrieval results with explicit confidence contracts.

## Evidence Classes

- Official evidence: records loaded from AEAT, BOE, CNMV, AEPD, ESMA, EUR-Lex, DGT, BORME, or other configured official workers with `source_url`, `boe_reference`, CELEX, or source identifier.
- Agent reasoning: synthesis, applicability analysis, condition mapping, or checklist generated by the model.
- Discovery: URLs found by search, SearXNG, or browsing. Discovery is not evidence until a worker verifies and loads it.
- Skill workflow: procedural guidance for analysis. It is never evidence.

## Red Lines

- Do not state a filing obligation unless returned evidence explicitly supports it for the user's facts.
- Do not fill gaps in FATCA/CRS, AEAT model instructions, BORME mercantile facts, or EUR-Lex article text from general knowledge.
- Do not cite a skill, prompt, or repo as legal authority.
- Do not treat `casillas_total > 0` as proof that a model applies to a taxpayer.

## Minimal Context Questions

Ask for facts only when needed: jurisdiction/residence, entity type, regulatory status, client type, transaction type/date, income type, withholding role, account type, and whether the user asks about filing mechanics, model applicability, or casilla inventory.
